Vehicle Manufacture Sample App
Welcome to the Vehicle Manufacture Sample Application. This was built using IBM Blockchain Platform: Develop, and has been deployed to the IBM Blockchain Platform running on IBM Cloud.
What can I do in this web application? This application showcases the simple benefits of the IBM Blockchain Platform within the scenario of buying and manufacturing a vehicle. You will be able to see the view of vehicle buyer: Arium Buy, the manufacturer: Arium Manufacture and regulator: Vehicle & Driver Authority (VDA).
